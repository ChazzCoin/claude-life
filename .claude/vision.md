# Vision

Long-arc direction. Where I'm going. This is the *destination*
that day-to-day actions get checked against.

> Different from values: values are how I move; vision is where
> I'm moving. Together they form the *aligned journey* — the
> through-line that makes daily actions feel coherent or
> dissonant.
